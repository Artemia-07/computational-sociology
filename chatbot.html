Chatbot Guide

Hello! Welcome to MindEase, a chatbot designed to provide you with a listening ear, helpful resources, and thoughtful responses on various mental health topics. This guide will help you understand how to interact with MindEase and get the most out of your experience.

MindEase can assist you in several ways. Here’s a quick overview of what you can ask:
- MindEase provides educational information on topics such as anxiety, depression, stress, and more.
- Ask MindEase for practical techniques to improve mental well-being, including breathing exercises, grounding techniques, or ways to boost your mood.
- MindEase can recommend books, articles, and websites for more in-depth information on mental health topics.

Sample Questions

"Do you have book recommendations on mindfulness?”

“What are some ways to stay positive?”

“Where can I learn more about self-care?”

“How can I manage my stress?”
